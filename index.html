ABOUT

Isabella Mullen is a master's student in Computational Social Science at U.C. San Diego. Her research focuses on developing innovative methodologies for ideal-point estimation in political science. Utilizing deep neural networks and normalizing flow models, Isabella integrates non-tabular and tabular data to map political positions into a low-dimensional latent space, advancing the precision and depth of political discourse analysis. She is passionate about taking an interdisciplinary and human-centered approach to data science.
